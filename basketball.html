BASKETBALL

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played between two sides of five players each. It was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, as a way to keep athletes active indoors during the winter.

The game quickly grew in popularity and spread worldwide. Today, basketball is one of the most-watched sports globally, with professional leagues like the NBA setting the standard for talent, athleticism, and entertainment.

The objective of basketball is simple — score points by shooting the ball through the opponent’s hoop. Speed, teamwork, skill, and strategy play a huge role in success. The game is known for its dramatic finishes and exciting plays.

Legendary players such as Michael Jordan, Kobe Bryant, LeBron James, and Stephen Curry have made basketball more than just a sport — it is a cultural phenomenon that inspires millions of fans across the globe.
