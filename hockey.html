HOCKEY

Hockey is a fast-paced team sport played on ice or on the field, depending on the variation. It is one of the most widely played sports, with millions of fans worldwide who enjoy its speed and excitement.

Field hockey has a long history in countries like India, Pakistan, and the Netherlands, while ice hockey is extremely popular in Canada, Russia, and northern European nations. Both versions require teamwork, strategy, and skillful control of the ball or puck.

The objective of hockey is to score goals by sending the ball or puck into the opponent's net. Players use sticks to pass, dribble, and shoot, while goalkeepers defend with quick reflexes and agility.

Legendary teams and players have made hockey a global phenomenon, inspiring future generations and giving the sport a permanent place in Olympic history.
