HISTORY   OF   SPORTS

Sports have been a part of human culture since ancient times. From the Olympic Games in Greece to traditional hunting and martial practices in Asia and Africa, people have always played games to test strength, skill, and teamwork.

Over time, sports evolved from local traditions to global competitions. Today, they are not just about physical ability but also about entertainment, health, and even international unity through major events like the Olympics and the World Cup.

Modern sports continue to inspire millions, promoting discipline, teamwork, and respect. They also reflect cultural identity and are enjoyed by both players and fans across the world.
