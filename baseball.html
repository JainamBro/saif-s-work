BASEBALL

Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport that is especially popular in the United States, Japan, and several Latin American countries. It is played between two teams of nine players each, taking turns batting and fielding.

A baseball game is played over nine innings, where teams try to score runs by hitting the ball and running around bases. It is a mix of strategy, skill, and teamwork, making it a unique and engaging sport for both players and fans.

Legends like Babe Ruth, Jackie Robinson, and Derek Jeter have left lasting legacies, inspiring future generations to embrace the spirit of baseball.
