BOXING

Boxing is one of the oldest and most respected combat sports in the world. It is a test of strength, skill, endurance, and strategy, where two fighters face each other using their fists under strict rules.

The origins of boxing can be traced back to ancient civilizations like Greece and Rome, where it was part of the Olympic Games. Modern boxing, however, developed in England in the 18th and 19th centuries with the introduction of rules and gloves.

Professional boxing has given the world legendary champions like Muhammad Ali, Mike Tyson, and Floyd Mayweather. Their achievements inside and outside the ring have made boxing a sport of global fame and inspiration.
